Circle of care serves older adults in Boulder County, who are low-income and can no longer safely drive. If you can still drive safely, you are welcome to join us as a volunteer so you can help drive others and go to our events.

Notice:

  1. Only seniors who have been fully vaccinated and who are willing to wear a mask can get a ride in our volunteers cars to an event.
  2. Seniors who are unvaccinated can have a tickets but they need to get there themselves, social distance, wear a mask and must arrive at least 30 minutes prior to the event and be prepared to wear a mask with no dispute in the Chautauqua auditorium or anywhere else where it is requested by the venue and to all Circle of Care events.
